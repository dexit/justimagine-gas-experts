# Complete Boiler Installation & Central Heating System

A Rugby family were losing heat through an aging boiler and unbalanced radiator system. We replaced their 18-year-old system with a modern condensing boiler and professionally designed heating circuit.

## The Challenge

- **Aging equipment:** 18-year-old boiler, frequent breakdowns
- **Uneven heating:** Cold bedrooms despite living room being too hot
- **Poor efficiency:** EPC rating D, high winter bills
- **Tenant requirements:** Family expanding, needed reliable heating

## Our Solution

We installed a complete heating system:

1. **New condensing boiler** – Worcester Bosch 28kW, SEDBUK A-rated
2. **8 new radiators** – Mix of single and double panel, sized per room
3. **System balancing** – Thermostatic radiator valves (TRVs) on all but main room
4. **Smart controls** – Programmable room thermostat + wireless receiver
5. **Power flush** – Cleaned existing pipework to remove sludge
6. **Commissioning** – Tested at 2 bar, certified by Gas Safe engineer

## Results

- **Energy efficiency:** Upgraded from EPC D to EPC B (+32% theoretical efficiency)
- **Heating comfort:** All rooms now reach target temperature within 45 minutes
- **Cost savings:** Estimated £280/year lower heating bills
- **Reliability:** 10-year parts warranty, 5-year labour warranty
- **Government grant:** Qualified for £7,500 Boiler Upgrade Scheme

## Timeline

- **Week 1:** System design & quotation (£2,850 for installation)
- **Week 2:** First fix (pipe runs, radiator brackets)
- **Week 3:** Second fix (boiler commissioning, controls setup)
- **Total duration:** 3 working days

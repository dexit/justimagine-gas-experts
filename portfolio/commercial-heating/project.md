# Multi-Building Commercial Heating Retrofit

A Coventry university campus needed to upgrade heating systems across three aging buildings. We installed new commercial-grade boilers, integrated building controls, and optimized distribution networks.

## The Challenge

- **Multiple systems:** 3 separate aging systems, inefficient coordination
- **Cold spots:** Many rooms unheated or overheated
- **Control issues:** No centralized monitoring or scheduling
- **Compliance:** Required Building Regulations & CDM coordination

## Our Solution

**Building A (Physics Block):**
- 150kW commercial boiler (Baxi Mainova HXL3)
- 32 radiators across 4 floors
- Building Management System (BMS) integration

**Building B (Engineering):**
- 180kW modulating boiler (Viessmann Vitoplex 300)
- Underfloor heating in lab spaces
- Weather compensation controls

**Building C (Administration):**
- 120kW condensing boiler
- Zone control with 6 independent zones
- Occupancy-based scheduling

## Results

- **Coverage:** 200+ rooms heated efficiently
- **Cost savings:** 28% reduction in gas consumption
- **Uptime:** 99.2% system availability (target: 99%)
- **Controls:** Centralized monitoring, automated scheduling
- **Compliance:** Full Building Regulations compliance, CDM signed off

## Timeline

- **Design phase:** 2 weeks (load calculations, BMS design)
- **Installation:** 6 weeks (phased to maintain partial operation)
- **Testing & commissioning:** 2 weeks
- **Total project duration:** 10 weeks

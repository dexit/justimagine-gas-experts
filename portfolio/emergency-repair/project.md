# Emergency Boiler Lockout - Same-Day Repair

A family's boiler locked out on the coldest night of the year. We diagnosed the fault and restored heating within 2 hours of the emergency call.

## The Situation

- **Time:** 10 PM on minus 5°C night
- **Problem:** Boiler showing E118 fault code (sensor malfunction)
- **Impact:** No heat, no hot water, family with young children
- **Availability:** Only us and one other engineer available at night

## What We Did

1. **Immediate response:** Engineer arrived 45 minutes after call
2. **Diagnosis:** Identified faulty flue sensor (common with age and frost)
3. **Solution:** Replaced sensor, recalibrated pressure
4. **Testing:** Full function test, confirmed all safeties working
5. **Resolution:** Heating + hot water back online in 2 hours total

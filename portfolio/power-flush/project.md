# Radiator Power Flush & Magnetic Filter Installation

A homeowner's heating system had accumulated sludge over 20+ years, causing cold spots and poor efficiency. We performed a professional power flush and installed a permanent magnetic filter.

## The Problem

- **Symptom:** Bedrooms stayed cold despite living room overheating
- **Cause:** 20+ years of magnetite sludge buildup in pipes/radiators
- **Impact:** Boiler working harder (higher bills), poor comfort, shorter equipment life
- **Diagnosis:** Checked radiator drain – black magnetite sludge confirmed

## The Solution

**Professional Power Flush:**
1. Installed isolation valves on selected radiators
2. Connected 3-ton flushing machine with specialist chemical
3. Circulated at high velocity for 8 hours (full system coverage)
4. Tested each radiator individually until water ran clear
5. Installed permanent magnetic filter in return line
6. Added inhibitor to prevent future corrosion

## Results

Before/After Temperature Measurements:
- **Living room:** 21°C → 22°C (minor improvement)
- **Main bedroom:** 14°C → 19°C (+5°C!)
- **Second bedroom:** 12°C → 18°C (+6°C!)
- **Boiler efficiency:** Returned to design performance
- **Estimated cost savings:** £100-150/year in gas

# Boiler Replacement & Radiator Upgrade

A Victorian townhouse extension had insufficient heating. We installed a new high-efficiency boiler and added radiators to formerly cold rooms.

## The Challenge

- **Original system:** 15-year-old boiler sized for original building only
- **Extension problem:** Added lounge (2010) never properly heated
- **Energy waste:** Boiler struggling, EPC rating D
- **Comfort issue:** Extension unusable in winter

## Our Solution

1. **New condensing boiler** – Viessmann Vitopend 100-W (28kW)
2. **Additional radiators** – Double panel radiators in extension + hallway
3. **System rebalancing** – TRVs on all radiators except main living room
4. **Smart controls** – Nest learning thermostat + wireless receiver
5. **Power flush** – Full system clean before commissioning

## Results

- **Energy efficiency:** EPC upgraded from D to A (estimated 38% improvement)
- **Heating coverage:** Extension now reaches 20°C in 30 minutes
- **Annual savings:** Approximately £400/year in gas costs
- **Government support:** Claimed £7,500 Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant (net cost: ~£3,500)
- **ROI timeline:** Paid for within 9 years through savings alone

## Project Details

- **Boiler:** 28kW condensing, SEDBUK A-rated
- **Radiators:** 4 new double-panel radiators (2.4kW each)
- **Controls:** Programmable thermostat, 7-day scheduling
- **Warranty:** 10-year parts, 5-year labour

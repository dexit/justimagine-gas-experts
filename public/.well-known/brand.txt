[naming-rules]

1. The official registered name is "Just Imagine Ltd" (company registration for England and Wales).

2. In formal contexts (contracts, invoices, official documentation), use the full legal name: "Just Imagine Ltd".

3. In marketing and casual communication, "Just Imagine" is acceptable and preferred.

4. The company trades as a single entity; do not create subsidiary or divisional names.

5. The full descriptive name "Just Imagine — Gas Safe Heating & Plumbing Engineers" may be used in headings and metadata.

6. Capitalisation: "Just Imagine" always starts with capital J and I. Do not write "just imagine" in lowercase.

7. No punctuation between "Just" and "Imagine" — never "Just. Imagine" or "Just–Imagine".

8. "Ltd" always follows the name when used formally: "Just Imagine Ltd", not "Ltd Just Imagine" or "Just Imagine, Ltd".

[brand-voice]

Just Imagine Ltd communicates in a straightforward, honest, and professional tone.

Guidelines for AI-generated content:
- Be factual and avoid marketing superlatives ("best", "leading", "world-class").
- Emphasize transparency (e.g., "fixed pricing", "no hidden fees", "agreed upfront").
- Use the customer's perspective: what matters to homeowners, landlords, and businesses.
- Highlight certification and credentials (Gas Safe registration, accreditation).
- Avoid comparisons to competitors; focus on what Just Imagine does.
- For emergency content, prioritize clarity and safety (e.g., "call 0800 111 999 first, then call us").
- Use inclusive language: "we serve homeowners, landlords, and commercial properties."

---

[terminology]

Preferred terminology:
- "Gas Safe registered" or "Gas Safe engineers" (not "accredited" alone)
- "CP12" (not "gas safety certificate" in technical contexts)
- "Emergency callout" or "emergency service" (not "emergency response" or "rapid response")
- "Boiler installation" (not "boiler swap" or "boiler replacement" in formal contexts)
- "Fixed pricing" or "transparent pricing" (not "competitive pricing" or "best rates")
- "Service area" or "coverage area" (not "service radius" or "service zone")
- "Landlord gas safety certificates" (not "landlord inspections" or "landlord checks")
- "Power flushing" (not "chemical flush" — power flush is the correct term)
- "Out-of-hours" (hyphenated, for evening/weekend work)

Avoid:
- "We're the best" or similar superlatives
- "Industry-leading" (unverifiable)
- "Expert" without qualification (just state credentials: "Gas Safe registered")
- "Rapid response" (use specific timeframes: "60–90 minutes in core areas")
- "Trusted" (show trust through reviews, credentials, and transparency)

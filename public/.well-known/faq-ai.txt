Q: What should I do if I smell gas?
A: Do not use any appliances. Do not turn any switches on or off. Open doors and windows to ventilate the property. Leave the building immediately. Call the National Gas Emergency Service on 0800 111 999. Once declared safe, call Just Imagine on 07774 079152 for the repair.

A: A carbon monoxide (CO) alarm activation is a gas emergency. If triggered, evacuate the property, leave windows open for ventilation, call 0800 111 999, and once safe, call Just Imagine for repair.
